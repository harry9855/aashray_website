About us

It is Non profitable

Welcome to AASHRAY The Service Club. This COVID-19 has been a time for immense hardship for a lot of people especially those who have lost their loved ones. COVID 19 has taken two of the most precious resources from us, life and time. While we at Aashray cannot bring back lost time but we can try to ease the stress that people face…… “Aashray” is a non-profit organisation that strives on helping people who have lost not just their near and dear ones but also their bread winners. Aashray’s goal is to try and provide financial support to as many people as possible.

How we work

Way of Work & requirement by needy.

Here at aashray we first created a funding source, namely crowd funding. We take the donated funds and source the basic resources (groceries, rent, electricity bill, water bill, etc.) and get them delivered to person who requested them. Aashray dosnt reveal the name or the photographs of the people we help, only their stories and why they needed what they needed.
